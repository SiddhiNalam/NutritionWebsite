How it works?



Its a 3 step process



1
THE PRE-CONSULTATION STAGE
  • You start by filling in the DAR (diet and activity) sheet for 3 days which will help us figure out your existing eating pattern, food likes- dislikes, nutrient & calorie intake, energy expenditure and how it changes during the day.
  • A pre-consultation document that sets down expectations from the program

2
CONSULTATION STAGE
  • You start off with an hour long first appointment where a meal plan and an exercise prescription will be prepared with your full involvement.
  • Weekly interactions at a designated time to review and alter your meal and exercise plans as necessary.
  • You'll get your diet plan via email which will be password protected. Once you make the payment,you will recieve the password to unlock it.
  • Phone, whatsapp and email support for all of your queries.
  • Special meal planning for special occasions like work travel, holiday, wedding, festivals, etc.

3
POST-CONSULTATION STAGE
  • Regardless of the duration of your program we maintain a lifelong relation with you and provide you support anytime you need.
  • A post- consultation document will summarize all the learnings from the program that we feel are important and will provide quick and practical food planning guidelines for most of the situations you will face.
